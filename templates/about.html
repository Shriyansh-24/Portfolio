About Me

Biology × Code × Track.
All in.

Hey — I'm Shriyansh. I'm in my final year of school in Doha, Qatar, studying Physics, Chemistry, Biology, AI, and PE. That's a weird mix, I know — but I think the most interesting things happen at the edges between fields.

My obsession is the space where biology and artificial intelligence collide. I've always been fascinated by the complexity of living systems — and now I get to use code to understand and augment them. From classifying DNA sequences to analyzing biomechanics with computer vision, every project I build answers a question that genuinely keeps me up at night.

Outside of code, I'm a national-level track athlete. Running taught me discipline, iteration, and how to be comfortable being bad at something until you're not. Those same principles apply to everything I build.

I call myself a polymath in progress — I want to learn everything: biology, CS, design, writing, finance. I think intellectual breadth is a superpower that most people underestimate.
