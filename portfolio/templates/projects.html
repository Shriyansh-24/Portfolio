My Philosophy

Why biology + AI?

Biology is the most complex information system that exists. It evolved over billions of years to solve problems we haven't even fully understood yet. AI gives us a new lens to look at that complexity — and new tools to work with it.

I don't think of myself as just a programmer or just a biology student. I think of myself as someone who translates between those two languages — asking biological questions and building computational answers.
